# data-analysis-global-superstore
# Project Overview
End-to-end Sales Performance Analysis using Python, SQL, and Looker Studio. The objective is to evaluate sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and market contribution while providing actionable business recommendations through SQL analysis, exploratory data analysis (EDA), forecasting, and an executive dashboard.
# Business Qustions
Management would like to answer the following questions:
* How are sales and profit performing?
* Is the business growing year-over-year?
* Which market and country contribute the most?
* Which product category generates the highest profit?
* Does discount affect profitability?
* Which customer segment creates the highest business value?
* Which countries require immediate attention due to negative profit?
* What is the projected sales trend for the next 12 months?
